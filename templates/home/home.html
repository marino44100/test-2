Tutors Finder System


The goal of the this system is to help students overcome academic challenges and lead them to autonomous or independent learning. It is a special kind of website that will help student to find the way of glorious achievements in life.

Services

Who Can Use

Catagory of users and their descriptions.

Educators Panal

An educator can register with his desirable subjects which he will be able to teach the students. He can also select his location, where he can find his students.

Parents Panal

A parent can search for a teacher  for his/her child. Here he/she can search by location and desirable subjects, then confirm the request towards the teacher.

What We Offer

We offer the following features for the users.

Search by location

Both tutors and parents can search by specific location of tution.

Find subjects

Parents can easily find for desirable subjects for their child.

Rating

Parents can give ratings to the tutors as their performance.

Home tutor

Users can find home tutors as their criteria, who will teach in the home.

Outside tutor

Users can search outside tutors, who teach in the outside along with many student

Available 24/7 days

Users can use this system 24 hours and 7 days in the week.
